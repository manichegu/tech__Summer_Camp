Introduction

Dear Parents and Guardians, We are excited to announce that our school will be hosting a Tech Summer Camp Program for students this year, in collaboration with Strategy-M incubated @ CBIT-ACIC.

The Summer camp will provide a fun-filled learning experience for students, with a range of activities designed in compliance to NEP 2020 , The Industrial Revolution 5.0 and The World Economic Forum to promote adaption of technology and innovative thinking among students. Registration is now open to reserve a spot for your child, please complete and submit the attached registration form along with the fee by 4th May 2023. We want students to participate and experience a Memorable, Exciting and Learning Summer Camp.
